# Q: What is the minimum sustainable tick rate for HF to maintain basic health monitoring?

## Answer
Based on the provided wiki pages, the minimum sustainable tick rate for HF to maintain basic health monitoring is **daily**.

The HF Daily Operating System emphasizes tracking a small set of core metrics every day, favoring consistency and repeatability over precision. The daily report template is designed to be filled out once per day and includes essential health signals like sleep, energy, mood, and activity. This daily cadence aligns with the goal of making health tracking sustainable and actionable without becoming burdensome [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md, hf-daily-report-template.md].
