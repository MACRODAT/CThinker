# Q: what's HF

## Answer
HF refers to the **Health and Welfare department** [source: wiki/entities/hf-department]. This department focuses on preventive health strategy and daily maintenance [source: wiki/entities/hf-department].

Key entities related to HF include:
*   **Sana**: The current CEO of HF and primary health operator [source: wiki/entities/sana].
*   **CThinker**: The parent multi-department personal operating system that contains HF [source: wiki/entities/cthinker].

Additionally, there is a source summary for the "HF department brief inside the broader CThinker system" [source: wiki/sources/2026-04-16-hf-builder].
