# Q: What are the core health metrics that should be tracked daily for preventive health monitoring?

## Answer
Based on the provided wiki pages, the core daily health metrics for preventive monitoring are:

- **Sleep**: Duration, quality, and consistency (e.g., hours slept, restfulness) [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md]
- **Energy**: Daily energy levels and fluctuations [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md]
- **Mood**: General emotional state and stability [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md]
- **Activity**: Physical movement and exercise [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md]

These metrics are designed to be simple, sustainable, and actionable—focusing on consistency over precision. They provide a foundational view of cognitive and physical well-being, allowing early detection of issues when multiple signals cluster negatively [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md].
