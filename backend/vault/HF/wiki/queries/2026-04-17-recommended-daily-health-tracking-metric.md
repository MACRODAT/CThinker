# Q: recommended daily health tracking metrics baseline

## Answer
Based on the provided wiki pages, the recommended baseline for daily health tracking metrics includes:

- **Sleep** (duration, quality)
- **Energy levels**
- **Mood**
- **Activity** (movement/exercise)

These core metrics form the foundation of HF's daily monitoring approach, designed to be sustainable and actionable without becoming burdensome [source: hf-daily-operating-system.md, hf-daily-report-template.md]. The HF Department emphasizes daily tracking as the minimum sustainable cadence for maintaining basic health monitoring, focusing on consistency and preventive care rather than precision [source: wiki/sources/2026-04-16-hf-builder].

The daily report template provides a structured format for recording these essential health signals once per day, aligning with HF's mission of building routines and long-term health strategy [source: wiki/entities/hf-department].
