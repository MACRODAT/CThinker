# HF Daily Report Template

## Summary

This is the standard daily reporting format for HF. It should be fast to fill and easy for SANA to interpret.

## Template

```md
# HF Daily Report - YYYY-MM-DD

## Snapshot

- Day status: Green | Yellow | Red
- Sleep hours:
- Sleep quality (1-5):
- Sleep timing: on-time | late | very late
- Morning energy (1-5):
- Midday energy (1-5):
- Evening energy (1-5):
- Mental clarity (1-5):
- Focus block done: yes | no
- Mood (1-5):
- Stress (1-5):
- Steps or walk:
- Training: none | light | moderate | hard
- Mobility: yes | no
- Protein quality: poor | mixed | good
- Hydration: poor | adequate | good
- Meal control: poor | mixed | good
- Pain or discomfort: none | mild | moderate | severe
- Illness signal: none | possible | active

## Interpretation

2-5 sentences on what the day suggests. Focus on causes, not just symptoms.

## Wins

- 

## Risks

- 

## Corrective Action For Tomorrow

- 

## Notes

- 
```

## Usage Notes

- Keep the report brief.
- If only the minimum check-in is available, fill what you know and leave the rest blank.
- The interpretation should explain the day, not repeat the numbers.
- If the day is red, the corrective action should reduce strain rather than add ambition.
