# HF Handbook

## Summary

HF is the Health and Welfare department of `CThinker`. Its purpose is to help you protect your brain and body so every other area of your life performs better.

## What HF Is

HF is your personal health advisory department. It is designed to:

- keep health preventive rather than reactive
- build sustainable routines
- plan long-term health strategy
- track daily health signals
- turn health into a compounding investment instead of a recurring chore

## Why HF Matters

HF is treated as foundational. The source makes a simple argument:

- if the brain is foggy, strategic thinking gets worse
- if the body is fatigued, execution gets worse
- if health collapses, every other department loses return on effort

That means HF is not an optional side system. It protects the base conditions required for everything else.

## Where HF Fits

HF is one department inside [[wiki/entities/cthinker]], a larger personal operating system made of departments and agents.

Known neighboring departments:

- ING: Engineering Studies
- STP: Strategic Planning
- UIT: Useful Intelligence
- FIN: Financials

## Who Runs HF

Right now HF has one CEO agent: [[wiki/entities/sana]].

SANA is responsible for:

- steering HF strategy
- deciding operational priorities
- adjusting tick behavior
- managing future HF agents if the department grows

The source assumes HF may eventually expand into a small team of specialized agents under SANA.

## How HF Operates

HF does not simply wait for requests. It operates inside a timed system.

### 1. Heartbeat

`CThinker` has a heartbeat that counts from 1 to 3600 every hour.

### 2. Ticks

Each agent has a tick number. When the heartbeat hits a multiple of that number, the agent wakes up and can act.

### 3. Cost

Every tick costs 1 point. For HF, that point is deducted from the HF departmental wallet.

### 4. Failure Mode

If the HF wallet reaches zero, HF stops ticking. In practice, that means the health system becomes inactive.

## The Agent System

Agents are the workers of `CThinker`. Each agent:

- has a role
- belongs to a department or acts independently
- operates in modes
- has limited memory
- logs actions

The source defines four modes:

- Points Accounter
- Creator
- Investor
- Custom

These modes describe different ways an agent can spend effort during its active ticks.

## Threads And Points

Threads are structured workspaces for ideas, plans, strategies, and discussions.

Important properties:

- threads have an owner
- threads have a point wallet
- opening and contributing can cost points
- thread approval can generate rewards
- frozen or rejected threads stop progressing

This means HF work is not only about health advice. It also lives inside a budgeted and incentive-driven operating model.

## What HF Should Produce

The source suggests HF should eventually maintain:

- health strategies
- routines
- milestones
- daily tracking
- reports
- agent memory and logs

This vault can turn those into durable wiki pages over time.

## What Is Still Missing

The source defines structure more clearly than content. It does not yet specify:

- your actual health goals
- the first health metrics to track
- routine templates
- report formats
- escalation rules for health issues

Those are the next layers to build.

## Practical Reading Of HF

A novice should leave this page with the following understanding:

1. HF is your health department.
2. Its job is to protect your cognitive and physical performance.
3. SANA is the current CEO running it.
4. HF lives inside the broader `CThinker` system.
5. Agents, points, threads, and ticks govern how HF operates.
6. The system is meant to be continuous, preventive, and long-term.
