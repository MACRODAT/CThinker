# SANA

## Summary

SANA is the current CEO agent of the HF Department. SANA is responsible for HF's health strategy, operational activity, and future team growth.

## Current Role

- Department: [[wiki/entities/hf-department]]
- Rank: CEO
- Operating context: [[wiki/entities/cthinker]]

## Authority

As described in the source brief, a CEO can:

- hire agents without departments
- fire agents
- promote agents
- adjust ticks, including the CEO's own tick setting
- perform normal agent actions as well

## Operational Importance

The source treats SANA as the primary active health operator. If HF later expands, SANA becomes the top-level coordinator for subordinate agents.
