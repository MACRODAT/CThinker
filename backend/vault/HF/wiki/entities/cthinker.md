# CThinker

## Summary

CThinker is the larger personal think-tank system within which HF operates. It organizes AI agents into departments and uses points, threads, logs, and incentives to structure work.

## Known Departments

- [[wiki/entities/hf-department]] for health and welfare
- ING for engineering studies
- STP for strategic planning
- UIT for useful intelligence
- FIN for financials

## Core System Model

- Agents belong to departments and spend points to act.
- Threads structure collaborative idea exchange.
- Departments and agents have wallets or budgets.
- Activity is scheduled through a heartbeat and tick mechanism.

## Relevance To HF

HF is not a standalone health note system. It is one department inside a broader multi-agent personal operating system.
