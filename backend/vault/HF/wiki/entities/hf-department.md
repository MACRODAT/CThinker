# HF Department

## Summary

HF is the Health and Welfare department inside `CThinker`. Its job is to protect and improve the user's health so the rest of the system can perform well.

## Mission

- Act as a personal health advisor.
- Shift health from a chore into a high-yield investment.
- Prioritize prevention over repair.
- Build stable healthy routines.
- Design long-term health strategies and milestones.
- Track health continuously at the daily level.

## Strategic Role

HF is treated as the base layer for all other departments. If health degrades, planning, finance, engineering, and intelligence work all lose effectiveness.
